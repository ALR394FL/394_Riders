Eligibility overview

Who can join?

American Legion Riders members must be current members in good standing of The American Legion, American Legion Auxiliary, or Sons of The American Legion.

Members must also own a legally registered motorcycle—individually or through marriage—and operators must be properly licensed and insured under state law.
